Biography

I am a recent graduate with a Master's in Mechanical Engineering specializing in robotics at the University of Colorado at Boulder. Originally from Mumbai, my ambitions have me placed halfway across the world learning how to build robots. Isaac Asimov, a Hugo award winner for the best novel, the Foundation Series, kindled my passion for becoming a roboticist. On a side note, I'm a huge fan of Anime and The Office show. During my breaks, I usually go for a run or play table tennis. Also, reading and cooking are frequent indulgences.
